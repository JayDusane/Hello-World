Our mission is to provide everyone a most useful Skill Development Program

Today’s market needs experience but, that does not quite work for newbies. So, here we are to present TendWe. Our aim is to provide an opportunity to every individual, where users can work with any companies on their freelance projects

It’s social media platform, we ask users to create a profile on TendWe. Once the user creates a profile, he can start with surfing through multinational companies where he can find numerous projects uploaded by the organization. In that project summary companies put every single detail on how they want to execute their project, what are the requirements etc., when users click on the project, they will ask to put their details such as Aadhar card/ passport details. Mobile number, name, date of birth. After they fill details users can pick the project and start working, once they done with the project, they have sent it back to the company via TendWe. And the reputed organization will review the project if their project fulfills companies' need then the company will give a rating to the Users profile. Based on that rating TendWe will calculate and give an overall score which we are going to name (TendWe score).

As many projects you do and if the company accept your work, TendWe will give you Experience/Accommodation letter. Which will be provided by you via a company with a respected owner or CEO’s signature. So, you can put that on your resume as a reference. There will one other thing which is the rating. When they came across a situation where they have to invest money to complete the project. So, every time the company gives you rating TendWe will pay you as appreciation. (5 to 6k as a stipend).
